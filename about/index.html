Adam Kaplan is a skilled front-end developer with experience in every portion of the design process, from wireframing to writing the actual code.

He built his own open-source responsive framework that optimizes websites for usability on all devices—from mobile phones to large screens—by responding to the user’s behavior, screen size, and screen orientation.

After graduating from Columbia College in 2004 with a Bachelor of Arts in film editing, Adam worked for several years as a film editor and production assistant before accepting a UX apprenticeship at 8th Light in January of 2013 under the direction of Stephanie Briones.

In his time at 8th Light, Adam has learned to keep things simple. By taking a detail-oriented approach to planning and mapping a logical web platform, Adam is able to focus on crafting elegant designs that prioritize usability before beauty.

Adam created and hosted a three-part design workshop alongside Stephanie Briones that walked beginning design students through the process of creating and designing their own blog.
