Born and raised in Chicago, Adam grew up with a love for creating things.

Before designing, he studied film at Columbia College and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts. He was a video editor for many years before discovering 8th Light.

Adam studied design under Stephanie Briones, where he learned to keep things simple. Adam believes that good design should not only be beautiful but also easy to use.

When he is not designing you will find him running, playing video games, and going out to dinner with his friends.
